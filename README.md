 📊 Bharti Airtel Financial Modeling & Analysis (2023–2027)

 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive financial analysis and 5-year financial model of **Bharti Airtel Limited. It includes a fully integrated 3-statement financial model (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, and Cash Flow Statement), along with forecasting, ratio analysis, and industry insights.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate practical skills in financial modeling, corporate finance, audit, and equity research.

---

 🏢 Company Overview

Bharti Airtel is one of India's leading telecommunications companies with operations across Asia and Africa. The company provides mobile services, broadband, enterprise solutions, and digital services.

---

📊 Key Features of the Model

 📈 5-Year Financial Forecast (FY2023–FY2027)
📄 Integrated 3-Statement Model:

   Income Statement (P&L)
   Balance Sheet
   Cash Flow Statement
 📉 Ratio Analysis:

   Profitability (ROE, ROA, EBITDA Margin)
   Liquidity (Current Ratio, Quick Ratio)
   Leverage (Debt/Equity, Interest Coverage Ratio)
 🔍 Scenario Analysis (Base, Bull, Bear Case)
 💰 Free Cash Flow (FCF) Analysis
 📊 Peer Comparison (Reliance Jio, Vodafone Idea)

---

 🧠 Key Assumptions

 Revenue growth driven by ARPU expansion and subscriber growth
 EBITDA margin expansion due to operating leverage
 Capex expected to decline post 5G rollout
 Gradual debt reduction through strong operating cash flows
 Telecom industry growth supported by data consumption and 5G monetization
